Labview, RS232 and USB
 
June 29, 2006
I have started to have a look at the book LabView for Everyone 2nd Ed by Jeffrey Travis. I see section 2.4 discusses RS232. From the perspective of this book I'd say the photogate boxes we are thinking of making would be considered RS-232 instruments.


"Serial port communication is also painstakingly slow..."
The validity of that statment depends on the amount of data that needs to be transfered. The PIC18F4525 can be set up  to communicate at 19200 bits per second with would be 1920 bytes per second. I think that is fast enough for what we will want to do.
