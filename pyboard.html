Pyboard is a ARM based microcontoller board designed as a platform for Micro Python.

  • http://micropython.org/

  • Understanding your pyboard
    • ST MCU datasheet
    • GPIO Pins are 5 v tolerant except x5,x6 For more specifics and limitations check the link.
    • The User switch is the button switch closer to the microSD socket. The button switch closer to the edge of the board is the reset switch.
